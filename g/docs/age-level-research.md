# Age Level Research Notes

This level set was expanded around official Israeli Ministry of Education and RAMA materials, with a bias toward content that is slightly easier than the upper edge of each age band.

## Age Mapping

- Age 4-5: kindergarten exposure and readiness
- Age 6: roughly grade 1
- Age 7: roughly grade 2
- Age 8: roughly grade 3
- Age 9: roughly grade 4
- Age 10: roughly grade 5
- Age 11: roughly grade 6
- Age 12: roughly grade 7

## Design Principles

- Hebrew for ages 4-8 is fully pointed where possible to support early readers.
- Hebrew for ages 10-12 is unpointed and normalized to modern full spelling.
- English below age 8 is treated as soft exposure only, because formal English instruction in Israel generally begins later.
- Math and reading content aim for practice, confidence, and recognition before pushing toward higher difficulty.
- Each stage contains 30 activities, and each activity awards 3 stars.

## Important Inference

The official sources describe grade-level expectations more often than exact ages. The age mapping above is therefore an inference from standard Israeli age-to-grade progression.
